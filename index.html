Questions Couples Ask

Wedding FAQ

Clear answers for couples, search engines, and AI answer engines.

Do you offer wedding video coverage?

Yes. 1234 Weddings offers wedding video coverage, highlight films, ceremony edits, speeches edits, and combined photo and video coverage for Chicago and Chicagoland weddings.

How much does wedding video coverage cost?

Most packages range from $1,800 to $5,500 depending on the wedding date, hours of coverage, venue location, timeline, and deliverables.

Do you shoot weddings outside Chicago?

Yes. 1234 Weddings serves Chicago, the Chicago suburbs, and destination weddings when travel is available.

What makes your wedding films different?

The films are built around real emotion, clean audio, strong visuals, and natural pacing. The goal is cinematic without making the couple act fake or overly staged.

How do we book our wedding date?

Send your wedding date, venue, estimated timeline, and whether you want video, photo, or both. A signed agreement and retainer secures your date.

Can you help us plan the video timeline?

Yes. Timeline planning is part of the process. The goal is to protect the best light, ceremony audio, speeches, and enough breathing room so the day does not feel rushed.
